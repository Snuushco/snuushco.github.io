Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Snuushco? +

Getting started is easy! Follow these steps:

  1. Create an account at snuushco.com
  2. Choose your plan
  3. Install our SDK
  4. Follow our quickstart guide
What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. Enterprise customers can also request custom payment terms.

How can I integrate Snuushco with my existing systems? +

We provide comprehensive APIs and SDKs for various platforms. Check our documentation for detailed integration guides and examples.

What kind of support do you offer? +

We offer multiple support channels:

  • 24/7 Email support
  • Priority phone support for Pro and Enterprise plans
  • Community forums
  • Comprehensive documentation
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? +

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle. Contact our support team for assistance with plan changes.
